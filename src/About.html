A Journey of Dedication

Hailing from a modest background, Dr. Ashwin Kumar's journey to becoming a cardiac expert started at Andhra Medical College, Visakhapatnam, where he earned his MBBS. His pursuit of excellence led him to complete his Post Graduation in Internal Medicine at Maharaja Agrasen Hospital, New Delhi. Noteworthy stints followed as a Senior Resident in the Department of Medicine at KGH and as a Senior Registrar in Cardiology at KIMS - ICON Hospital.

A Quest for Expertise

As an Assistant Professor at NIMS, Hyderabad, and KGH, Visakhapatnam. Dr. Kumar imparted knowledge to aspiring cardiologists and continually honed his craft. His academic contributions and clinical acumen were pivotal in shaping the future of cardiac care. He strived to improve cardiovascular health outcomes through rigorous research, innovative treatments, and compassionate patient interactions.

Compassionate Care in Practice

Currently, Dr. Ashwin Kumar serves as a Consultant Interventional Cardiologist at Medicover, Visakhapatnam, where he is known for his expertise in advanced cardiac procedures and patient-focused care. His exceptional skills in interventional cardiology, coupled with his compassionate approach, have earned him a stellar reputation among patients and peers alike.

In addition to his primary role, Dr. Kumar extends his expertise as a Visiting Consultant at HAL Hospital, Sunabeda, where he provides specialized cardiac care and collaborates with a multidisciplinary team to enhance patient outcomes. His commitment to delivering high-quality, evidence-based treatments underscores his dedication to the field of cardiology.

Beyond his clinical responsibilities, Dr. Kumar is the Founder and Director of Dr. Panda's Heart Care, a premier cardiac wellness center. This institution is a beacon of hope for those seeking comprehensive cardiac wellness, offering a range of services from preventive cardiology to complex interventional procedures. Under his leadership, Dr. Panda's Heart Care emphasizes a holistic approach to heart health, integrating lifestyle modifications, cutting-edge technology, and personalized treatment plans.

A Personalized and Holistic Approach

Dr. Ashwin Kumar's approach to cardiology is deeply rooted in genuine care and empathy. Recognizing that each patient is unique, he listens intently, taking the time to understand the full spectrum of his patients' health. This patient-centered methodology ensures that every concern, no matter how small, finds a compassionate ear.

Dr. Kumar's philosophy goes beyond merely treating medical conditions; his goal is to empower individuals with the knowledge and support they need for a healthy life. He believes in educating his patients about their health, offering clear explanations and guidance on managing and preventing heart disease. This educational aspect is a cornerstone of his practice, as he aims to equip his patients with the tools they need to take proactive steps towards maintaining their cardiovascular health.

Dr. Kumar's commitment to personalized care is evident in his thorough assessments and tailored treatment plans, which address not only the symptoms but also the underlying causes of cardiac issues. By fostering a supportive and nurturing environment, he builds strong doctor-patient relationships based on trust and mutual respect.
